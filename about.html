I took my first pottery class in January 2020. At the time, I was drained, tired, and unfulfilled from my uninspiring day job. I was immediately hooked and signed up for a membership to spend more time with clay on my own, to develop a craft on my own time. And thus, nthspace was born.

Pottery is a medium in which I channel my creative energy into something physical and tangible. I hope that the joy I feel in making each piece is captured in the clay and carried into your home.




nthspace (n.) - The endless, infinite spaces we can occupy. The limitless possibilities each of us possess.
